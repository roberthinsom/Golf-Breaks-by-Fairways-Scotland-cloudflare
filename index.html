Top Golf Break Packages in Scotland: Combining Luxury and Championship Courses

Scotland is globally recognized as the birthplace of golf, with its rich golfing heritage and stunning courses. From the historic Old Course at St. Andrews to the rugged beauty of the Highlands, Scotland boasts some of the world’s finest golf courses. If you’re a golf enthusiast looking to enjoy an unforgettable experience that combines luxury with top-tier golfing, then a golf break in Scotland is the perfect getaway. Whether you’re seeking to improve your swing, savor world-class cuisine, or unwind in a lavish hotel, Scotland offers golf packages that suit every taste and budget. For expertly curated golf breaks, check out Golf Breaks by Fairways Scotland, where you can explore the best golf courses and luxurious accommodations tailored to your preferences. Here are some of the best and most unique golf break packages in Scotland, where luxury meets championship golf.

1. The St. Andrews Experience: Golf at the Home of Golf

Location: St. Andrews, Fife

Golf Courses: The Old Course, The New Course, The Castle Course

Why Choose This Package: No golf break in Scotland would be complete without a pilgrimage to St. Andrews, the birthplace of golf. This package allows golfers to play on the legendary Old Course, a bucket-list course for any enthusiast. The experience includes access to several world-renowned courses, including the more challenging New Course and the spectacular Castle Course overlooking the coastline.

Luxury Add-ons:

  • Stay at the iconic Old Course Hotel, a 5-star property with stunning views of the Old Course and the surrounding landscape.
  • Enjoy personalized golf lessons or a caddy service to guide you through the historic fairways.
  • Indulge in gourmet dining at The Road Hole Restaurant, offering modern Scottish cuisine with a panoramic view of the golf course.

Package Perks:

  • Exclusive behind-the-scenes access to St. Andrews’ golf history with private tours of the R&A Golf Clubhouse and the British Golf Museum.
  • Complimentary spa access to unwind after a long day of golfing.

2. The Highlands Golf Retreat: A Tranquil Escape with Scenic Views

Location: Inverness, Highlands

Golf Courses: Royal Dornoch, Nairn Golf Club, Castle Stuart

Why Choose This Package: The Highlands of Scotland provide a golf experience that is as much about the breathtaking scenery as it is about the game itself. This package includes tee times at three of Scotland’s most prestigious courses, including the renowned Royal Dornoch and the stunning Castle Stuart, both offering exceptional links-style golf.

Luxury Add-ons:

  • Stay at the Cromarty House Hotel, a luxurious boutique hotel that combines modern comfort with traditional Scottish charm.
  • After a day on the course, enjoy a private whisky tasting session, where you can sample the finest Highland malts.
  • Access to a private chauffeur service for comfortable transfers between courses and nearby attractions.

Package Perks:

  • Guided tours of historic castles and monuments in the area.
  • A dedicated concierge to tailor your itinerary with non-golfing activities like hiking, fishing, or spa treatments.

3. The Ayrshire Coastal Golf Tour: A Blend of Links and Luxury

Location: Ayrshire, Southwest Scotland

Golf Courses: Royal Troon, Turnberry, Western Gailes

Why Choose This Package: Ayrshire is home to some of Scotland’s most prestigious links courses, including the world-renowned Royal Troon and the scenic Turnberry. This golf break package combines some of the best links golf in the world with luxurious accommodation in a picturesque coastal setting. Turnberry’s Ailsa Course, often ranked among the best in the world, promises a challenge for golfers of all levels.

Luxury Add-ons:

  • Stay at the Trump Turnberry Resort, which offers unrivaled luxury with breathtaking views over the Atlantic Ocean and the famous lighthouse.
  • Tee off in style with a private helicopter transfer from Glasgow to Turnberry for a truly unique experience.
  • Savor a fine dining experience at the 1906 Restaurant, offering exquisite Scottish cuisine using locally sourced ingredients.

Package Perks:

  • Access to the exclusive Turnberry Spa, featuring luxurious treatments designed to relax your muscles after a day on the course.
  • VIP access to the Ailsa Course for an enhanced golfing experience.
